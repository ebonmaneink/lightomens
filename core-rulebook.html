Welcome to character creation! In this chapter, you will learn how to create your own unique character for Light Omens, including selecting your origin, archetype, and talents.

Attributes

A character’s attributes represent their core abilities, showing how capable they are in specific actions. Each attribute is rated from 1 to 6.

Body Attribute

The Body attribute represents a character's physical prowess, such as strength, agility, and endurance. It is a measure of their physical strength, dexterity, and stamina.

Example actions: Lifting heavy objects, balancing on a beam, swimming long distances.

Mind Attribute

The Mind attribute represents a character’s intelligence, wisdom, and concentration. It measures their cognitive abilities, problem-solving skills, and mental strength.

Example actions: Solving puzzles, perceiving traps, quick thinking.

Soul Attribute

The Soul attribute reflects a character’s ability to be convincing, resolute, and calm under pressure. It also measures their honesty and social skills.

Example actions: Intimidating enemies, deceiving guards, remaining calm in danger.

Attribute Scale

Attribute Rating Description
1 Weak Attribute
2 Decent Attribute
3 Strong Attribute
4 Exceptional Attribute
5 Outstanding Attribute
6 Legendary Attribute

Approaches

A character’s approaches reflect their training and understanding in specific areas, indicating how effective they are at completing certain actions. Each approach is rated from 0 to 4.

Force Approaches

Force approaches focus on direct actions, taking advantage of obvious opportunities and simple solutions.

Finesse Approaches

Finesse approaches emphasize careful planning and skillful tactics, preferring indirect methods.

Focus Approaches

Focus approaches rely on patience, endurance, and mental fortitude, remaining steady through difficult challenges.

Approach Scale

Approach Rating Description
0 Untrained Approach
1 Competent Approach
2 Skillful Approach
3 Expert Approach
4 Masterful Approach
