Light Eater

Type: Light Eater

Difficulty: Hard

Stats and Abilities

The Light Eater is a fearsome entity that thrives in the dark corners of Hiraeth. Its ability to drain the light and sap the strength of its foes makes it a formidable adversary.

  • HP: 250
  • Attack: 35
  • Special Ability: Light Drain

Encounter Suggestions

Light Eaters often appear in dark dungeons or shadow-infested lands. When using this creature in an encounter, pair it with shadow minions to increase its lethality.
