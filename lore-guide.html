Explore the World of Hiraeth

Region 1: The Shattered Wastes

The Shattered Wastes are a desolate and dangerous region, where few dare to tread. Ruins of an ancient civilization dot the landscape, and the Grim Light’s influence is strongest here.

Factions in this Region

The Revenites

The Revenites are a faction of fallen warriors, bound to the land by the Grim Light’s power. They are a ruthless and dangerous force.

Region 2: The Verdant Plains

A vibrant and lush area of Hiraeth, the Verdant Plains are home to numerous factions, all vying for control over the fertile lands.

Factions in this Region

The Technocrats

The Technocrats are a highly organized faction focused on technology and innovation, seeking to bring order to Hiraeth through mechanical and magical means.
