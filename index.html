What is Light Omens?

Light Omens is a tabletop role-playing game set in the world of Hiraeth, where hope battles against the encroaching Grim Light. Players take on the roles of adventurers striving to navigate a world teetering on the edge of darkness.

Dive deep into rich lore, challenging mechanics, and a vivid world filled with endless possibilities. Whether you're a veteran or new to the game, Light Omens offers something for everyone.
